Shipping Information

Shipping Information for WAK Publishers Books:

We understand the excitement of receiving your new book and diving into its pages. We strive to make the shipping process as smooth and efficient as possible, ensuring that your order arrives promptly and in pristine condition. Here’s everything you need to know about our shipping procedures:

Shipping Options: We offer several shipping options to cater to your needs:

Standard Shipping: Our standard shipping option delivers your order within 3-5 business days. This option is perfect for customers who are not in a rush to receive their books.

Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are calculated based on the delivery location, selected shipping method, and the weight of the items in your order. You can view the shipping costs for your specific order during the checkout process. We strive to offer competitive shipping rates while ensuring the safe and timely delivery of your books.

Order Processing Time: Once you place your order, our team works diligently to process it as quickly as possible. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Please note that processing times may be slightly longer during peak seasons or promotional periods.

Order Tracking: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email. If you want to track your order contact us at wakpublishers@gmail.com or +92 328 6042977.

Shipping Partners: We partner with reputable shipping carriers to ensure the timely and secure delivery of your books. Our shipping partners include Pakistan Post and sometimes TCS. Rest assured that your order is in good hands from the moment it leaves our warehouse until it reaches your doorstep.

We are committed to providing exceptional service at every step of your shopping experience, including shipping. If you have any further questions or need assistance with your order, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our friendly customer support team.
